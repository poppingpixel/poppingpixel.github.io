### Machine Learning and Hardware for Sustainability

A combined showcase of earlier hardware-software co-design research aiming at sustainable energy generation and consumption:

#### 1. Energy Management Using Machine Learning

- Implements smart grid optimization using prediction models to balance supply and demand.
- Integrates anomaly detection to prevent power distribution failures.

#### 2. Open Gym Electricity Generator

- Designs a kinetic energy harvesting system coupled with a gym workstation.
- Implements a localized power metrics logger.
